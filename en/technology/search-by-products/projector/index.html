3LCD Projection Technology

3LCD projectors can produce amazingly bright and beautiful images by the differences between 3LCD and the other main projection technology.

Laser Light Source Technology

The 3LCD method is a method in which images of red, green, and blue created using three liquid crystal displays are combined with a dichroic prism and projected.

Microdisplay Technology

Projects bright images by combining images of red, green, and blue,which are created using three liquid crystal displays, with a dichroic prism.

ike standard-throw projectors, Ultra-short throw projectors can cast a large image even when installed a very short distance from a wall or screen.

Enriching lives with projector technologies

Leveraging laser light sources to expand into new areas

Laser projectors produce bright, vivid images, have a long-lasting light source, and can be installed at any angle. Epson will accelerate business growth in the high-lumen segment by evolving laser light source engine platforms to efficiently expand and upgrade the product lineup.

To allow people to enjoy big-screen entertainment at home, we will expand and upgrade the lineup of compact models and ultra-short throw models, giving them attractive designs that blend into the home interior.

We will also highlight the advantages of projectors to create demand for accent lighting projectors for use in stores, restaurants, and entertainment venues.
