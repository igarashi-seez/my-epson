Manufacturing Technologies

Manufacturing and design technologies

Thin-Film Piezoelectric Technology

Fabricates piezoelectric elements on silicon wafers by depositing uniform, homogeneous films a mere micron in thickness.

MEMS Fabrication Technology

Builds mechanical components, sensors, actuators, electronic circuits, and more on a single silicon substrate with sub-micron precision.

Silicon MEMS Technology

Epson's silicon MEMS technology makes it possible to form complex 3D structures on silicon substrates.

High-Quality Synthetic Crystals

One of the world's largest crystal device manufacturers, Epson also manufacturers its own crystals.

PrecisionCore Printhead Manufacturing Technology

A combination of inkjet, MEMS, and robot technologies enables both outstanding quality and productivity.

Metal Injection Molding (MIM)

MIM is a process with that has numerous outstanding features, including the ability to create complex three-dimensional shapes, a high degree of freedom in use of materials, and suitability for volume production.

Smaller Display Modules

Smaller, lighter display modules make it possible to view perceived big-screen images wherever you want, stress-free.

Smart Glasses Optical Technology

See-through smart glasses are compact and lightweight yet deliver clear big-screen images.
