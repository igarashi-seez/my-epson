Emerging Technologies

PrecisionCore

PrecisionCore enables digital printing of amazing images at blazing speeds. It saves resources and delivers gorgeous output with virtually no waste to respond to the growing demand for short-run print jobs in the industrial and commercial sectors.

Force Sensors

Manufacturers are facing labor shortages and numerous other issues such as passing down know-how to young generations. Epson is helping to address these issues by using force sensors to automate complex, precision tasks that formerly relied on handwork.

Sensing Solutions

Sensing solutions that detect changes in speed, orientation, and direction in three dimensions help make our lives more convenient and safer.
