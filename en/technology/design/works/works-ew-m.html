EW-M670FT / EW-M571T

Inkjet Printer

2017

New Value Created by Functional Beauty That Puts Your Mind at Ease During Use

The EW-M670FT and EW-M571T are A4 inkjet multifunction printers with high-capacity EcoTanks.
In order to meet the needs of customers who want to print a lot at a low cost, we have adopted a high-capacity EcoTank system, where the user fills the ink directly into the tank of the product itself. In order to eliminate the worries of spillage and hassles during ink filling, we have pursued operability that "is easy, provides peace of mind, and helps prevent spills." It also expresses the performance of the EcoTank while maintaining its harmony with the atmosphere. We have achieved both beauty and functionality.
We uncovered the latent needs of our customers and thoroughly eliminated their concerns to create a printer with a completely new form.

Unprecedented operability that "is easy, provides peace of mind, and helps prevent spills"

We aimed to make it as easy as a shape-matching puzzle that young children play with. We changed the shape of the bottle tip for each color to prevent mistakes when pouring in ink. Also, as inserting the bottle automatically starts the filling process, and the process automatically stops when the tank is full, there is no need to worry about the ink overflowing. Furthermore, the valve structure at the bottle tip prevents ink from spilling even when the bottle is upside down.

It thoroughly eliminates the worries you feel when using it, and provides you with the peace of mind of an unprecedented, comfortable operating experience.

Appearance of high printing capacity

The tank that protrudes from the unit front expresses the performance of the large-capacity EcoTank while harmonizing with the main unit. By using a simple round shape and a weighty appearance, we have achieved both the durability to withstand long-term use and harmony with the atmosphere.

Compact even with a large-capacity EcoTank

By placing the tank on the front of the main unit, we not only made it easier to fill the ink and see the remaining ink level, we also achieved a compact size almost the same as the ink cartridge model. The flat top surface was surface-treated to make scratches and dust less noticeable, and it provides a temporary place to store documents or printed materials so you can effectively use the limited space around your desk.
