Inkjet Printer

2016

Designed like an interior accessory that you'll want in your living room

The printers we deliver are not simply machines for printing. They are designed to be tools used in family communication spaces that are a part of your daily life.
Unlike conventional "devices," the shape of this product is wrapped in soft curves that will make you want to touch it. While it has an endearing presence, it also exudes a comfortable atmosphere where it is placed.

Pursuing simplicity while maintaining ease of use

The structure of the moving parts tends to become complicated, so we reviewed it to pursue simplicity. The power button, which is the first point of contact when using the device, is a perfect circle LED floating on a neat surface, giving an endearing sense that is also fully controlled precision.

Natural feel, natural usage

The flat top surface features an embossed dot matte. The calm and natural feel not only blends in with the interior design, but is also functional in making scratches and dust less noticeable, and is designed to accompany natural movements, like placing a camera or papers on it like a table.

More freedom in placement

Like a bowl that has no front, back, left, or right, we designed all sides of the product as the front. No matter where it is placed or from what angle it is viewed, you will feel the full appeal of its design.
